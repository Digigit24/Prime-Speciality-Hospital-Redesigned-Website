## Laparoscopy

n

Laparoscopy is **a procedure used to check the organs in the belly (abdomen)**. It can also check a woman's pelvic organs. Laparoscopy uses a thin lighted tube that has a video camera. The tube is called a laparoscope.

### Benefits of Laparoscopic surgery

* Signs of very minor injuries, and less tissue cutting.
* You will experience less pain while the scar heals, which results in less usage of pain relievers.
* There will be quick healing as the scar is small.
* As the exposure of internal organs to external pollutants decreases, the risk of infections decreases.
* The recovery period after laparoscopic surgery is about 1 to 2 weeks.
