#### **Breast diseases**

* Breast lump - fibroadenomas, fibroadenosis, benign breast disease
* Phylloids tumour etc.
* Breast abscess/mastitis
* Nipple discharge
* Breast cancer

#### 

#### **When to consult**

* Common symptoms
* Palpable lump in breast
* Pain in breast
* Painful Swelling in breast
* Nipple discharge
* Puckering of skin over breast
