# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Patients in the Moshi, Bhosari, and PCMC areas seeking expert laparoscopic, general, and proctology laser surgical care. They are looking for clear clinical information, credentials of the surgeon, and a seamless way to request appointments.

## Product Purpose
Clinical marketing and registration portal for Prime Speciality Hospital under Dr. Pankaj Bhalerao. The website exists to build trust, present surgical information clearly, and enable patients to contact the clinical coordinators easily.

## Brand Personality
Expert, clinical, reassuring, premium, modern-yet-accessible.

## Anti-references
SaaS-cliché landing pages, ghost-card borders paired with heavy shadows, cheap emoji lists, hard-to-click hover menus, and low-contrast typography.

## Design Principles
1. **Expert Confidence:** Focus on credentials, facilities, and clear data rather than sales-pitchy copy.
2. **Clear Spacing Rhythm:** Dynamic padding and clean gutters that adapt from mobile to wide screen viewports.
3. **Intentional Motion:** Smooth scroll reveals that enhance rather than gate visibility, with support for prefers-reduced-motion.
4. **Clean Interactive Hierarchy:** Accessible menus, input fields, and layouts that work flawlessly for keyboard, touch, and screen readers.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Targeting WCAG 2.2 AA compliance. Ensure correct contrast ratios for body text and placeholders, full keyboard accessibility on interactive elements, and immediate layout stability when animations are disabled.
